About BGSA The Bangladeshi Graduate Student Association (BGSA) is a student-led organization at The Ohio State University dedicated to building a strong community among Bangladeshi graduate students, promoting cultural exchange, and supporting academic and professional development.

Our Mission

We aim to create a welcoming environment for new and current Bangladeshi students, provide mentorship opportunities, and celebrate Bangladeshi culture through social and cultural events. BGSA also serves as a bridge between students and alumni to strengthen academic, research, and professional connections.

The association actively participates in cultural programs, volunteering, and collaboration with other student organizations across the university. Together, we represent the vibrant Bangladeshi community within the OSU family.

Our Initiatives BGSA organizes academic, social, and cultural programs throughout the year to engage students and celebrate Bangladeshi heritage.

Cultural Night

An annual evening of music, dance, and traditional food, celebrating Bangladeshi culture with students, alumni, and friends of OSU.

New Student Orientation

Helping new Bangladeshi graduate students settle into Columbus life — from academic tips to local guidance and cultural connection.

Alumni Networking

Building professional bridges between current students and BGSA alumni to foster collaboration and career development.
