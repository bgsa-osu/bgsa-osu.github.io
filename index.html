About BGSA The Bangladeshi Graduate Student Association (BGSA) at The Ohio State University is a community of graduate students, scholars, and alumni of Bangladeshi origin fostering cultural connection, collaboration, and support among members.

Who We Are

BGSA@OSU works to promote cultural awareness, provide academic and social support, and strengthen ties between Bangladeshi students, alumni, and the wider OSU community.

We host events, celebrate festivals, and assist new students in adapting to graduate life at OSU. Our goal is to build bridges between current students and alumni to create a lasting professional and personal network.
